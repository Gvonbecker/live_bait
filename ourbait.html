Choosing The Right Bait

When it comes to deciding how to choose bait for fishing, there are certain things to consider

1. Consider what fish eat. Live(or natural) baits are anyhting alive or previusly alive

2. Select good saltwater baits including sea worms, eels, crabs, shrimp,strips of squid.

3. Always check your fishing regulations to make sure the bait you choose is legal

4. Check your budget.

5. Learn about the difference between naturl bait vs artifical.
